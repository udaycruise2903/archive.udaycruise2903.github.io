Monoids and Semigroups

  1. (head . tail) tail computes the third element of a list.
  2. Lists are an instance of Semigroup with (++) as (<>): [1] <> [2,3] => [1,2,3]

  3. #+beginsrc haskell data Sum a = Sum a instance Num a => Semigroup (Sum a) where Sum a <> Sum b = Sum (a + b)

    data Product a = Product a instance Num a => Semigroup (Product a) where Product a <> Product b = Product (a * b) #+endsrc
